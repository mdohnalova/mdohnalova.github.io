Efficient Logistics System (ELS)

Tracks shipment status in real time and flags a problem before it turns into a lost order.

Problem

A customer never picks up their package, or a carrier reports damage — and the business only finds out once the customer starts complaining. Nobody has time to manually check hundreds of shipments for what's at risk.

Solution

I built a dashboard that watches every shipment's status for me. When a package sits at a pickup point too long, it prepares a discount-code reminder automatically. When a carrier reports damage, one click sets up a replacement shipment and an apology discount — and nothing goes out until I approve it myself.

Proof of concept built on sample shipment data — real carrier integration is still waiting for the right opportunity.

Key Benefits
  • 🎯 Risky shipments show up in one queue, not scattered across carrier spreadsheets.
  • 🎟️ Reminder and apology codes are ready with a click, not typed by hand in Excel.
  • 📦 A damaged shipment and its customer apology are handled in seconds.
  • ✅ Nothing goes out without my approval — I stay in full control of customer communication.

Data Cleaner

Cleans up nonsense in product names and generates description copy for me.

Problem

A product export from an old e-shop is full of marketing clutter — "#1 BEST SELLER!", discount percentages baked into the name, declined filler phrases. Manually rewriting hundreds of listings for the new platform would take days.

Solution

The tool first mechanically strips symbols, ranking prefixes, and discount phrases from the name, then runs it through AI once more to catch what the rules missed — generating a sales description plus three SEO keywords along the way. Only the name and description ever reach the AI, never stock or price data.

Key Benefits
  • 🧹 Clutter disappears from product names automatically, no manual rewriting.
  • ✍️ A description and SEO keywords are ready in seconds per product.
  • 🔒 Only the name and description text reach the AI — sensitive data never leaves the sheet.
  • 📋 I can still fine-tune every result in a table before exporting.

Hungarian Mail

Helps me understand Hungarian emails and reply in Hungarian, even though I don't speak the language.

Problem

I don't speak Hungarian, but orders from Hungary still come in. Hiring a native speaker just for occasional email traffic isn't worth it, and translating sentence by sentence in Google Translate is slow and unreliable.

Solution

I paste in the Hungarian email, the app translates it, and AI drafts a Czech reply along with a read on the message's tone (formal, urgent, friendly, complaint). I can tweak the reply or regenerate it in a different tone, and once I'm happy with it, I translate it back into Hungarian — nothing sends automatically, I just copy it into my email.

Key Benefits
  • 🇭🇺 I understand a Hungarian email instantly, no dictionary and no native speaker needed.
  • 🎛️ I can have the reply drafted in whatever tone fits the situation.
  • ✍️ I always edit the final text myself before sending — AI only prepares the draft.
  • 📤 The finished Hungarian reply copies straight into my email in a couple of clicks.

Customer Support Sidekick

🚧 Still in the works.

Problem

Support gets dozens of messages a day and has to manually figure out what's an urgent complaint versus a routine stock question, and what tone to reply in. Without a system, an angry customer can end up waiting in the same queue as a routine question.

Solution

AI sorts every incoming message by type, priority, and customer mood, and drafts a reply right away. The operator sees a clear, priority-sorted queue, picks a ticket, edits the suggested reply, and only then sends it.

Key Benefits
  • 🎯 Every message is automatically categorized by type and priority, no manual sorting.
  • 🚨 An angry customer or urgent complaint doesn't get lost among routine questions.
  • ✍️ A reply draft is ready to go — I just fine-tune it and send.
  • 📊 Processing speed and customer sentiment are visible in one place.
